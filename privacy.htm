Privacy Policy

Privacy Statement

We take individual privacy seriously and are also aware of the threats to your security and privacy on the Internet. Please take a few moments to review the steps we take to ensure your privacy and security while giving you the best possible customer service as you use our web site.

Ordering Online

All financial data is submitted directly to Paypal and is subject to Paypal's privacy policy and security. Paypal then sends us your contact information (but no financial information) so we can send you the books that you have ordered.

We do not sell or rent your name or any of your other contact information to anyone. We may use your name and address to ask for your satisfaction with your order. And, although we disagree with it, we must turn over any pertinent information to any law enforcement agency that takes the proper steps to require it.
